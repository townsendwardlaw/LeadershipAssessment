⬛ Performance — What they deliver today
1. How consistently does this person meet or exceed the goals and KPIs set for their role?
2. How would you rate the quality and reliability of this person's output and decisions?
3. How effectively does this person execute against the company's strategic priorities — not just their own function?
4. How would you describe this person's impact on the overall team and culture — do they raise the bar or create drag?
◆ Potential — Their capacity to grow and lead
5. How quickly does this person learn, adapt, and apply new skills or knowledge — especially under pressure?
6. How well does this person respond to critical feedback? Do they act on it, dismiss it, or get defensive?
7. Does this person think and communicate at a strategic level — beyond their own function and role?
8. If this role became significantly more complex or senior, how ready do you believe this person is to step into it?
