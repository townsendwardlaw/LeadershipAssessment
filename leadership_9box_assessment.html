Tier 1 — Knockout Assessment
8 high-signal questions. Choose the option that most accurately reflects observable behavior — not hope or potential.
Performance & Reliability
1. If this executive left tomorrow, what would immediately decline?
This surfaces the real size of their footprint versus their title.
2. Over the past 12 months, how consistently have they delivered on their commitments?
3. Do they solve problems, or do they generate problems for others to solve?
4. Would you confidently assign them a mission-critical initiative with real downside risk?
Leadership Capacity & Potential
5. When conditions change significantly, how do they respond?
6. Do they develop the leaders beneath them, or remain the primary decision-maker?
7. When they contribute to executive discussions, is it primarily strategic or operational?
8. Compared to 12 months ago, where is this executive?
